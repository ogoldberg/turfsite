What we need from you:

If you are interested in playing at the Turf Club, please provide as much of the following information as you can:

1) Do you have an order of preference for the dates you are interested in?

2) What is your website? Does it have MP3s? If you don't have a website, or it doesn't have mp3s, can we expect a demo from you at the Turf Club?

3) Are there any bands you would especially like to play with? Are you willing/able to communicate with them?

4) Would you like the show to be a special event (cd release, fund raiser, tour kickoff, reunion, etc.)?

5) How long has your band been together? Do you have members or ex-members of other bands worth mentioning?

6) How much work can you put into promotions (posters, flyers, etc.)?

NOTE: DO NOT promote or post a show to your myspace/website until it has been CONFIRMED with the responsible booking person!!!
