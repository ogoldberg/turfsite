The Turf Club is located 2 blocks North of I-94 and the Snelling Avenue Exit in Saint Paul. From the East or West on I-94 take the Snelling Ave Exit and go North on Snelling. In 2 blocks, at University Avenue, turn Left. The Turf Club is immediately on the right at 1601 University Ave next to the CVS Store. Bands should find the alley behind the venue for loading in the back door.
